Terms of the Participation Agreement

Ownership and control. Steven Scheurich (Lead Investor) independently purchased shares of Fervo Energy, Inc. (FRVO) through his personal Merrill Lynch brokerage account in connection with the company's initial public offering. The Lead Investor is and shall remain the sole purchaser of record, sole legal and beneficial owner of all shares unless and until any future transfer is lawfully completed. All voting rights, trading authority, and shareholder rights remain exclusively with the Lead Investor until a lawful transfer is completed.

Your participation right. You contributed funds to the Lead Investor in exchange for a contractual future participation right tied to the economic performance of the number of FRVO shares designated above. This agreement does not presently grant you legal title, brokerage account ownership, or direct shareholder rights unless and until a future transfer is completed.

Future transfer. Following expiration of any applicable brokerage or administrative restrictions, the Lead Investor intends and agrees to transfer shares corresponding to your participation interest through a direct brokerage-to-brokerage in-kind transfer. If an in-kind transfer cannot be completed for reasons outside the Lead Investor's control, the fallback is a cash payment equal to fair market value on the date of the attempted transfer, net of applicable taxes at the Lead Investor's rate. No specific transfer date is guaranteed.

Transfer timeline. November 2026 — collect brokerage details; December 2026 — begin initial transfers; January–March 2027 — complete remaining transfers in an orderly manner. Transfers will be staggered for administrative and compliance reasons.

Compliance. Nothing in this agreement is intended to circumvent any applicable securities laws, FINRA rules, AML requirements, KYC obligations, or brokerage compliance procedures. This arrangement is private and was not offered to the general public.

Death or incapacity. This agreement shall be binding upon the Lead Investor's estate, and transfers shall be completed by his estate or successor in the ordinary course.

Disputes. The parties agree to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation before pursuing legal remedies. Governed by the laws of the State of Louisiana, venue in St. Tammany Parish.

Tax matters. Each party is solely responsible for independent legal, accounting, and tax advice. Transferred shares are expected to carry the original acquisition date of May 12, 2026 and original cost basis of $27.00/share. After transfer, you are solely responsible for all future tax reporting and financial consequences.

Risk. FRVO is a speculative investment. Substantial losses, including total loss of capital, are possible. No guarantee has been made regarding future performance.
