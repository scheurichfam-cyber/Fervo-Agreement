Terms of the Participation Agreement

Ownership and control. Steven Scheurich (Lead Investor) independently purchased shares of Fervo Energy, Inc. (FRVO) through his personal Merrill Lynch brokerage account in connection with the company's initial public offering. The Lead Investor is and shall remain the sole purchaser of record, sole brokerage account holder, and sole legal and beneficial owner of all shares unless and until any future transfer is lawfully completed. All voting rights, trading authority, account authority, and shareholder rights remain exclusively with the Lead Investor until a lawful transfer is completed.

Your participation right. You contributed funds to the Lead Investor in exchange for a contractual future participation right tied to the economic performance of the number of FRVO shares designated above. This agreement establishes the parties' contractual rights and expectations. It does not presently grant you legal title, brokerage account ownership, or direct shareholder rights with respect to the FRVO shares unless and until a future transfer is completed.

Future transfer. Following the expiration of any applicable brokerage or administrative restrictions, the Lead Investor intends and agrees to transfer shares corresponding to your participation interest through a direct brokerage-to-brokerage in-kind transfer. If an in-kind share transfer cannot be completed for reasons outside the Lead Investor's control, the fallback is a cash payment equal to the fair market value of the shares on the date of the attempted transfer, net of any applicable taxes owed by the Lead Investor at his applicable tax rate. The Lead Investor retains sole discretion over transfer timing, subject to the transfer schedule described below, and no specific transfer date is guaranteed.

Transfer timeline. The anticipated schedule is: November 2026 — collect brokerage account details and confirm instructions; December 2026 — begin initial transfers; January through March 2027 — complete remaining transfers in an orderly manner. Transfers will be staggered for administrative and compliance reasons. This does not affect your rights under this agreement.

Compliance. The FRVO shares were purchased solely through the Lead Investor's personal brokerage account. Nothing in this agreement is intended to circumvent any applicable securities laws, FINRA rules, anti-money laundering requirements, know-your-customer obligations, or brokerage compliance procedures. This arrangement is private in nature and was not offered or solicited to the general public.

Death or incapacity. In the event of the Lead Investor's death or incapacity prior to completion of transfers, this agreement shall be binding upon his estate, and transfers shall be completed by his estate or successor in the ordinary course.

Disputes. The parties agree to resolve any dispute arising from this agreement through good-faith negotiation before pursuing any legal remedy. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Louisiana, with venue in St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana.

Tax matters. Each party is solely responsible for obtaining independent legal, accounting, and tax advice regarding this arrangement and any future transfer or disposition. The transferred shares are expected to carry the original acquisition date of May 12, 2026 and original cost basis of $27.00 per share, subject to brokerage processing procedures and applicable tax reporting rules. After transfer, you are solely responsible for all future tax reporting, capital gains or losses, and all other financial consequences associated with your shares.

Risk. FRVO is a speculative investment. The company is pre-revenue and in early-stage commercial deployment. Substantial losses, including total loss of capital, are possible. No representation or guarantee has been made regarding future performance or returns.
